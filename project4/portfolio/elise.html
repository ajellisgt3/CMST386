Lotus Elise Model Update

This is part of a mod project in which I'm setting out to redo the car models in Need for Speed: Hot Pursuit 2.

The base model for this Elise was taken from NFS Most Wanted and then edited to match the 2001 Series 2 Elise. I baked ambient occlusion into the skin texture, which matches the HP2 art style by mimicking what the original developers of the game did with their models. I also used Blender to paint the custom "Need for Speed edition" skin, which is based on the PS2 version of the game.
