Porsche 944 "Unleashed"

This is a modern adaptation of the main menu environment from Need for Speed: Porsche Unleashed.

The car model is heavily modified and pieced from various source models. Most of my work went into modeling the front and rear fenders, front bumper, and front nose. I also overhauled the gaps between body panels to replicate the art style of late BlackBox-era Need for Speed games (ProStreet/Undercover), in which I intended to use this model as a mod. It is derived from my 924 project, since both cars are based on the same platform.

Model sources include:

  • Forza Motorsport 7 - Porsche 924 Carrera GT (base model, better proportion accuracy than Forza's 944 model)
  • Forza Motorsport 4 - Porsche 944 Turbo (door handle, mirrors, front bumper. All heavily edited to fit this model).
  • NFSMW Mod - wheel model (heavily edited by me), fog lights/turn signal textures
  • NFS ProStreet - Tire textures

The environment model was ripped from NFS Porsche Unleashed, with lights placed in Blender.

This car modeling project is still very much a work in progress.
