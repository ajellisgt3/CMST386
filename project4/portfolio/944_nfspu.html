Porsche 944 Model Update

An update to the Porsche 944 Turbo model in Need for Speed: Porsche Unleashed. Also includes the 944 S2 and 944 Turbo S.

This project is similar to what I did for the Lotus Elise for Need for Speed: Hot Pursuit 2, except this mod uses the game's original textures and tries to keep the polycount down, similar to the original game.

I tried my hand at photomatching, mostly just to verify accuracy of my model, which was based on the other, higher-poly 944 project in this portfolio. Most of the wheels for each model were modeled from scratch.
